Design and Analysis of Various Engine Components

In this project, various components of engine such as cylinder liner, piston assembly, connecting rod, crankshaft, rocker arm, spring, exhaust valve are designed as per the requirement. A solid model of the components was designed in CAD software-Solidworks and stress analysis was done in Analysis software-Ansys. Check ou the cad models on drive.
